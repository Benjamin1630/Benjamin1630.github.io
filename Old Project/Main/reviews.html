Reviews

Below you'll find our Reviews on Games we have played in the past.

Minecraft

9/10

"Creative Freedom is Endless"

-Touch Digital


Minecraft is a game nearly everyone has heard of by now. You are dropped right into a World once you start and a goal or mission is never given to the player. This is makes learning the mechanics of the game feel much more rewarding and keeps the player dedicated. It is infamous for it's player-base being children, due to it's simple block style, making it easy for kids to learn. However, this game has so much more hidden in plain sight. With tons of items to craft and recipies to brew, this game has hours of endless enjoyment.






Absolver

8.5/10

"A Stunningly detailed Dungeon Crawler"

-Touch Digital


This game takes its player to a stunning world with breathtaking views. Don't let this fool you, as enemies are quick to overwhelm and attack you. The game gives the player a brief tutorial on the combat system which is extremely satisfying, as the player feels every punch and kick. Overall, this game recieves a 8.5 thanks to its beautiful scenery and brilliant combat.






Rainbow Six Siege

9.5/10

"A breath of fresh air."

-Touch Digital


This Ubisoft title is unlike any other game Ubisoft has produced in recent years. Siege has a large impact on the player, from strategically selecting operators for certain situations and map layouts to using technology such as drones to plan out your assault. Siege currently has over 20 operators as of now, all of which have their own unique abilities that they can bring to the table.






Red Dead Redemption 2

10/10

"A new Frontier in Storytelling"

-Touch Digital


This instant hit brings an old-timey world into modern technology. From the second it starts it makes the player feel as if we they in this dense world and never, not even for one second, does anything seem unreal. The graphics are stunning! This game keeps the player involved even with it's side missions which don't lack in quality. Rockstar has outdone themselves with this one!






Necropolis

8/10

"A great Dungeon Crawler"

-Touch Digital


This early access title is surprisingly polished with new enemies and weapons always revealing themselves to the player along their playthrough. The further down you go, the tougher it gets. This is apparent immediately with "Tiers". The higher the tier, the stronger it is. Levels remain exciting to play as different biomes and enemies keep the player busy and don't feel repetitive, since each level is procedurally generated to some degree.

**WARNING** Developers have abandoned this game. It's a shame.
