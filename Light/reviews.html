Below you'll find our Reviews on Games we have played in the past.

Red Dead Redemption 2

10/10

"A new Frontier in Storytelling"

-Touch Digital


This instant Hit brings an old-timey world into modern technology. From the second it starts you feel as if you were in this dense world and never for one second does anything seem unreal to you. This Game keeps you involved even with it's side missions which don't lack when it comes to Quality. Rockstar has outdone themselves with this one!




Necropolis

9/10

"A great Dungeon Crawler"

-Touch Digital


This early access Title is surprisingly polished with new enemies and weapons always revealing themselves to you along your playthrough. The further down you go, the tougher it gets. This is apparent immediately with "Tiers". The higher the tier, the stronger it is. Levels remain exciting to play as different biomes and levels keep the player busy and don't feel repetitive, since each level is procedurally generated to some degree.
