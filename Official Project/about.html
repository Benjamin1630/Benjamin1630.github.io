About Dream Corp LLC

Dream Corp LLC is an absurd workplace comedy series set in a neglected dream therapy facility. A rotating cast of desperate patients have their dreams recorded and analyzed by Dr Roberts and his unremarkable staff.

The series is the first on Adult Swim to use rotoscoping to achieve animation; this technique entails drawing over live-action footage.
