Basic rundown of our ESports Event.

We are Level 3 Games Development at BucksCollegeGroup on the Aylesbury campus. We would like to invite you to our ESports event on the 7th of January 2021.



FAQ

--------------------------------------------------------------------------

What game will be used?

We are making our own racing game. Don't expect too much.


How Many people will be allowed to attend?

As many as 17 will be allowed in one room at a time, however people can still watch and take turns playing the game. So we will be swapping out people constantly as to make sure everyone gets a proper go at the game we have made.



Can I take part in the event?

If you are Level 1, 2, 3 or extended and bear an orange card holder for your college ID, then you may partake in the event. Sadly, due to COVID-19, others will not be allowed to participate.



What measure will be taken to ensure the following of COVID guidelines?

The queue will be kept with a 2m distance between attendees and maschines will be wiped down after every use.


How long will the event run for?

The event will run the whole day from 9 until 5 on the 7th of January 2021.


Can I play against my friends?

Sadly, the game will be singleplayer, however, you may enlist with 3 others on the Sign Up page to play at the same time as them.


I am not available that day, what other options do I have?

Luckily for you, we are streaming the entire event online. You can find the link to the youtube/twitch stream at the top of this page when the day comes.
